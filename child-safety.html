Child Safety Standards

Last updated: April 3, 2026

PingUp ("the App") is a location-based social app intended exclusively for users aged 18 and older. We are committed to maintaining a safe environment and have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that sexually exploits or abuses minors (CSAE/CSAM).

1. Age Requirement

Users must be 18 years of age or older to register and use PingUp. We collect birth year during profile setup to enforce this requirement. Accounts found to belong to minors are immediately terminated.

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

  • Any content that sexually exploits or abuses minors is strictly prohibited.
  • Solicitation of minors for sexual purposes is prohibited.
  • Sharing, distributing, or linking to child sexual abuse material (CSAM) is prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to relevant authorities.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app using the in-app report feature available on every user profile. Reports are reviewed promptly and appropriate action is taken, including account suspension and law enforcement referral where required.

To report concerns outside the app, contact us at: zvenue176@gmail.com

4. Compliance with Local Laws

PingUp complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations. In the event that CSAM is discovered, we will report it to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and cooperate fully with local and national law enforcement authorities.

5. Enforcement

Violations of this policy result in immediate and permanent account termination. We reserve the right to report offenders to appropriate governmental and law enforcement agencies.
